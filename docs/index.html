Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Evenways ("the app") is a bill-splitting app for iPhone. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains — in plain language — how the app handles information. The short version: Evenways does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

Data We Collect

None. Evenways does not collect any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

Data You Enter

Any information you enter — such as restaurant names, the names of people in your group, receipt items, and amounts — is stored only on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This data never leaves your iPhone and is never sent to us or to any third party.

If you delete the app, this locally stored data is removed along with it.

Sharing

When you choose to share a split (as text or as an image) using the iOS share sheet, you decide where it goes. That action is handled entirely by iOS and the destination you pick (Messages, Mail, etc.). Evenways does not send this content anywhere on its own.

Third Parties

Evenways contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising services. There are no external services receiving your data.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data, it poses no specific privacy risk to children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
